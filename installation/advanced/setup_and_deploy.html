Advanced Setup and Deploy

This page guides discusses the preparation, configuration, installation and maintenance of a Cider-CI Environment. It covers many advanced topics. If you are new to Cider-CI or all you need is one single machine for occasional tests consider to digest the Easy Setup and Deploy guide.

This guide is optimized for easiness and providing quick results. After providing the prerequisites it will just take 3 easy steps and about three minutes your time to kick off the installation process.
